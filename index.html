How to Play Flip It

  • Aim of the game is to match all the pairs as quick as you can
  • Click a card to reveal the image underneath, then click another card to try and find the matching image
  • Once you click the first card, the timer will begin
  • Once pairs are found they will remain open on the page
  • After each round click Reset Cards, to shuffle the deck

Click anywhere on this page to get to the game

So off you go, it's time to get matching!
